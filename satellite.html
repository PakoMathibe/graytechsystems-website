FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is satellite internet available in my area?

Yes! Satellite internet is available anywhere in South Africa with a clear view of the southern sky.

How does weather affect satellite internet?

Modern satellite technology is very reliable. Heavy rain may cause temporary slowdowns, but service typically remains available.

What equipment is included?

Each package includes a satellite dish, modem/router, all necessary cables, and professional installation.

Can I use satellite internet for gaming?

Satellite internet has higher latency than fibre, so it's not ideal for competitive gaming. It's perfect for browsing, streaming, and working remotely.
